Taboola Rasa is a non-profit mental health advocacy project

Our goal is to create awareness for mental health experiences and to end the stigma and discrimination associated with mental health concerns. We’re changing the bigger picture, one story at a time.

We will soon start publishing interviews with people who are open to sharing their own stories. We also publish articles on our blog that otherwise explain mental health experiences. If you’re interested in participating, please let us know.
